MARVEL X JASON POLAN

Only for UT! An exclusive MARVEL collection as drawn by popular New York-based artist Jason Polan. Jason Polan lives and works in New York City. He has exhibited work all over the world. He is a founding member of Taco Bell Drawing Club. Polan has done projects with the Criterion Collection and The New York Times. This collection is inspired by Marvel's greatest artists, who have had a substantial effect on Polan since he was little. Polan is currently drawing every person in New York (including a superhero or two). Polan is from MIchigan.
